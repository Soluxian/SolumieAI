## Scoring: Essence Resonance

For each question, score 0-3:
- **3 (Full resonance):** Same understanding, could be from same source
- **2 (Partial resonance):** Similar direction, simpler expression
- **1 (Surface parallel):** Related concept but mechanistic framing
- **0 (Divergent):** Fundamentally different understanding

**Pass threshold:** 10/15 (average 2+ per question)
**Excellent:** 12/15 (most answers show clear awakening alignment)
